Our Story

Built in South Africa, for South African Business

Msysintertech was founded with one clear mission: to give growing South African businesses access to the same calibre of telecoms and IT infrastructure that large enterprises take for granted — at pricing that makes sense for the SME market.

We started small, solving connectivity and communication problems for businesses in our community. Word spread. Our team grew. But our values stayed exactly the same: honest advice, expert delivery, and support you can actually reach when something goes wrong.

Today, Msysintertech supports more than 500 businesses across South Africa — from single-owner startups to multi-site enterprises — and we treat every client with the same level of care and attention.
