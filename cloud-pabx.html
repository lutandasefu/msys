What Is Cloud PABX?

A Phone System That Lives in the Cloud

A traditional PABX requires an expensive on-site hardware unit, dedicated cabling, and specialist maintenance. Cloud PABX delivers exactly the same functionality — and much more — over your existing internet connection.

You get a fully featured phone system without the capital outlay. Add or remove extensions in minutes. Enable staff to work from anywhere. Pay only for what you use, monthly.

☁️ Most businesses save 40–70% on their phone system costs when switching from on-premise PABX to a cloud solution.

Features

Everything a Modern Phone System Needs

Unlimited Extensions

Add, remove, and move extensions at any time from the web portal — no engineer needed.

Auto-Attendant (IVR)

Professional greetings with intelligent multi-level call routing menus for any business structure.

Call Recording

Automatic cloud-stored call recordings for compliance, training, and quality assurance purposes.

Live Dashboards

Real-time call centre dashboards showing active calls, queue depths, and agent availability.

Mobile Integration

Your office extension rings on your smartphone — answer and make calls from anywhere as if you're in the office.

Voicemail to Email

Missed calls deliver audio voicemails directly to your inbox so you never miss an important message.

Conference Bridges

Dedicated conference bridge numbers for team and client meetings — no third-party service required.

Business Continuity

If your office loses power or internet, calls automatically redirect to mobile numbers or another site.
