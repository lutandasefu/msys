Our Process

From Survey to Signal in 4 Steps

1

Site Survey

We conduct a professional RF site survey to map your space, identify interference sources, and determine optimal access point placement.

2

Network Design

Our engineers produce a detailed wireless design including coverage maps, hardware specifications, SSID structure, and security configuration.

3

Installation

Certified installers deploy and configure all access points, switches, and controllers — with minimal disruption to your business operations.

4

Managed Support

Ongoing monitoring, firmware updates, and performance optimisation keeps your wireless network running at peak performance indefinitely.
